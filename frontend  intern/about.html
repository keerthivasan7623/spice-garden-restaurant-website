About Spice Garden

A modern South Indian restaurant built around family recipes, fresh ingredients, and generous service.

Our Story

Spice Garden began as a small breakfast kitchen serving crisp dosas and filter coffee. Today, we welcome guests for traditional South Indian meals, biryanis, breads, desserts, and celebration orders.

Every dish is prepared with warm spices, fresh curry leaves, house-made chutneys, and a simple promise: food should feel comforting, clean, and memorable.
